ABOUT SGGSIE&T

Shri Guru Gobind Singhji Institute of Engineering and Technology (SGGSIE&T), Nanded, is a premier autonomous institute committed to nurturing technically competent, socially responsible and globally aware professionals.

With strong research culture, industry collaborations and holistic student development, the institute blends academic excellence with leadership, creativity and innovation.

UTSAV AT A GLANCE

UTSAV’26 is the much-anticipated state-level intercollegiate cultural festival hosted by SGGSIE&T, Nanded, bringing together students from across Maharashtra.

A grand celebration of music, dance, drama, fine arts and cultural brilliance — where talent meets opportunity.
